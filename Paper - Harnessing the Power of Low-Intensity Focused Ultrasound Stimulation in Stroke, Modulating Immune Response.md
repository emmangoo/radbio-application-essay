- General ideas: radiation can be used therapeutically in diseases and conditions other than cancer or tumourigenic conditions, eg Stroke (This paper)
- here, both ischemic and hemorrhagic stroke treatments using radiation are described
- significantly, this method uses non-ionising radiation (ultrasound)
- here, low-intensity focused ultrasound stimulation was used to regulate inflammation post-stroke
- Like in tumour treatments frequency, duration, intensity, etc of the radiation are carefully considered
