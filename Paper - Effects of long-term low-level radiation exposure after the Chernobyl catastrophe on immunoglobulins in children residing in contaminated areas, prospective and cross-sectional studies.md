https://link.springer.com/article/10.1186/1476-069x-13-36

- focus of the paper is investigation of 137-Cs (main radioactive isotope from Chernobyl, originating from decay of Xenon) on Igs A, G, M, and specific E in children
- "the immune system in children whose parents resided in the third and second zones was characterized by similar changes" <= imbalanced immune status
- The question whether the imbalanced immune system itself causes higher disease risk or risk for other health complications remains
- again the changes in the immune system vary by dose and length of exposure 
- no clear linear associations, but some changes observed in Igs 6-12 years after the nuclear incident
